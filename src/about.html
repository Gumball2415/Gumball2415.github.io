about this site

this is a website.
here, you'll see some of my stuff.
this is my website.

a lot of stylistic choices were influenced by my early days of HTMLDev and old websites in general, it had this very charming yet simplistic design that was limited mainly by bandwidth and browser feature set at the time. this was also inspired by emma essex's webpage, which was my main inspiration for this style.

the backbone of the design was mostly from old code that i found lying about in my hard drive for a school project. after refactoring and touching up the jank, it becomes what you see now.

about me

hello, i'm persune. (pɜːsuːneɪ) i'm an aspiring video editor / chiptunist / musician / hardware-hobbyist / software-developer. more hobbies might come under my toolbelt as time passes by. in my free time, i usually work on my projects:

the name "persune" literally came to me in a dream. the words flashed in neon green, "who is Persune?". it was some kind of premonition i guess, because a week later, this video released and the imagery was exactly like the one in the dream.

i maintain Dn-Famitracker, a 3rd-derivative fork of FamiTracker, which contains emulation improvements, QoL improvements, and more.

i also make scripts from time to time whenever i need them, for different purposes.

i make little NES programs to learn 2A03 assembly, and i make chiptune music for the NES/Famicom.

i make Famicom cartridge PCBs just for learning, aiming to create an intricate expansion audio addresser for my chiptune music.

sometimes, i knit very long scarves, with the aim of each new one to get more complex in design.

my "finished" music goes through my record label " Sattelite & Stardust Recordings ", but other than that, i usually post my stuff on my youtube, twitter, or github.

i find it desirable to create something, but after some time passes, it gets quite boring, hence the shifting hobbies.
